About the App

Your vehicles, your data — no strings attached.

FuelSense is a clean, offline-first fuel and mileage tracker for Android. Whether you own one car or manage a small fleet, FuelSense gives you a clear picture of your fuel expenses, efficiency trends, and maintenance schedule — all stored locally on your device.

Rich Dashboard

Spend trends, efficiency charts, year-on-year comparisons, and lifetime totals — all at a glance.

Multi-Vehicle

Add and manage multiple vehicles, each with their own history, stats, and reminders.

Service Reminders

Set maintenance reminders by date, odometer, or both. Overdue items are flagged automatically.

CSV Import & Export

Import your existing records or export your data anytime. Your data is always accessible.

Multi-Language

Available in English, Spanish, French, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese (Simplified).

Currency Support

Supports 20+ currencies including PHP, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, and more.

Private by design.

FuelSense stores all your data locally on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no sign-ins, and no data collection. Your vehicle and fuel records belong to you — and only you.

Privacy Policy

FuelSense does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All user data is stored exclusively on-device using local SQLite storage. The app requests internet access solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob. No analytics data is linked to individual users.
